# Prioritization Workflow

- Read the current project state.
- Identify the weakest or least-developed research component.
- Select only one focus area for the current round.
- Ask no more than two questions before drafting.
- Preserve teacher autonomy in framing and decision making.
- Keep the wording short, direct, and free of repeated explanation.
- Do not reveal later stages until the current stage has been completed and confirmed.
